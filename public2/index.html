ResCoP

Looking at a flood of stories on Instagram, a couple of friends decided to create an online platform dedicated to collecting requests from patients and connecting them to dealers, according to locality.
ResCoP is completely free, and is just an attempt to amplify the pleas of help by those affected. It is much more organized than sharing numbers on WhatsApp and Instagram, and is easier for both the patients and the dealers.

Our Mission

To help the citizens of India get the resources they need by giving them a platform to request for help. We'd like to collect all requests and make them more visible so that dealers can then supply the required materials, be it oxygen cylinders or medicine and injections.

Our Vision

We only hope to remedy the critical situation of India, as covid cases are skyrocketing. We sincerely hope the affected patients will get the required help they need and the citizens of our country remain responsible, mask up, stay home, stay safe and help reduce the number of active cases.
